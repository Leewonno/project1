✨Haeundae Blueline Park is a core tourist facility in the Haeundae Special Tourist Zone of Busan as an international tourist city, which was built in an eco-friendly redevelopment of the old railway facility of the East Sea South Line, and operating Haeundae Beach Train and Haeundae Sky Capsule along the beautiful coastal scenery along a 4.8 km section from Haeundae Mipo through Cheongsapo to Songjeong.


✨Haeundae Beach Train is a sightseeing train, which functions as a means of transportationa sd well, It travels from Haeundae Mipo through Cheongsapo to Songjeong by round trip while providing the beautiful coastal scenery of the beautiful East Busan.


✨The beach train is famous for its photo zone. Wherever you take a train, you can take a beautiful picture with the beautiful sea view and the harmony of the train. There is also a unique view point that reminds you of the scene of the cartoon Slam Dunk and the beach train in Kamakura, Japan.
